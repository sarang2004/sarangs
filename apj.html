About The Legend

  • A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India
  • He served as the 11th President of Indian from 2002 to 2007
  • Kalam earned a degree in aeronautical engineering from Madras Institude of Technology and in 195 joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)
  • In 1969 he moved to Indian Space Research Organisation, where he was project the Director of SLV-3, the first satellite launch vehicle that was both designed and produced in India
  • Rejoining DRDO in 1982, Kalam planned the program that produced a number of successful missiles, which helped earn him the nickname"Missle Man"
